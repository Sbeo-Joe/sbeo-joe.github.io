OpenAI has launched native integrations connecting **ChatGPT** directly to **Uber**, **DoorDash**, **Spotify**, **Canva**, **Figma**, and **Expedia** — and is now reportedly planning to fold its **Sora** video generation model into the same interface, transforming the chatbot into an all-in-one <span class="hl-blue">AI operating layer</span>.

## Key Takeaways

- Six major apps are now natively integrated into ChatGPT — users can order food, book rides, control music, and generate designs without switching apps
- **Sora**, OpenAI's video generation model, is moving from a standalone app into ChatGPT's core experience, addressing falling engagement since its September 2025 launch
- OpenAI is executing a platform strategy that could shift the demand-generation relationship away from individual apps and toward ChatGPT itself

## What ChatGPT Can Now Actually Do

The integrations move ChatGPT from an information tool toward an action-execution layer. Concretely:

- 🚗 **Uber**: Request rides directly through a conversation
- 🍔 **DoorDash**: Order food without opening a separate app
- 🎵 **Spotify**: Control playback, get recommendations, build playlists
- 🎨 **Canva / Figma**: Generate and iterate design assets inside the chat
- ✈️ **Expedia**: Search and book travel end-to-end

This is the mature version of the plugin infrastructure OpenAI launched in 2023. The earlier system required deliberate activation and felt like a bolt-on. These integrations are native — the conversational interface itself becomes the transaction layer.

The behavioral shift being targeted: instead of "I need to open DoorDash," users start thinking "I'll tell ChatGPT what I want for dinner." Whoever intercepts that moment of intent owns the commercial relationship.

## Sora Moves In: Why the Standalone App Struggled

**Sora 2** launched in <mark>September 2025</mark> as a dedicated application and generated significant press attention. But usage fell as users encountered generation limits and the novelty of a separate video app faded.

The standalone app model required users to change their workflow — to think about video generation as a distinct, separate task. Moving Sora into ChatGPT removes that friction entirely. A user can describe what they want, iterate via text, and generate a video in a single continuous conversation.

> The standalone Sora app was seen as a smash hit when it launched alongside Sora 2 in September 2025, but interest has fallen as users ran into limits on generation.

## The Platform Play: ChatGPT as the New Home Screen

OpenAI is attempting to make ChatGPT the <span class="hl-blue">default interface layer</span> through which users access services — a role previously owned by Google Search, the smartphone home screen, and voice assistants like Alexa.

This creates a structural tension with the integrated partners:

- <span class="hl-blue">Short-term benefit for Uber / DoorDash</span>: access to ChatGPT's user base, new demand generation channel
- <span class="hl-red">Long-term risk</span>: customers may stop forming direct brand relationships — they'll just "ask ChatGPT" for a ride or a meal, with the underlying service becoming interchangeable

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform | Entry Point | Key Integrations |
|----------|-------------|------------------|
| OpenAI ChatGPT | Chat-first | Uber, DoorDash, Spotify, Canva, Figma, Expedia |
| Google Gemini | Search-first | Gmail, Docs, Google services |
| Apple Intelligence | OS-level | System-wide, third-party deep links |
| Amazon Alexa+ | Voice-first | Amazon e-commerce, smart home |

<span class="hl-red">The risk for OpenAI</span> is that chat is not the natural interface for every action — booking a complex multi-leg flight via conversation may be inferior to Expedia's dedicated UI. The integrations succeed if the conversational layer is genuinely easier than the alternative.

## Outlook

The first wave of plugin data from 2023 showed that most users did not habitually use external integrations. Whether native integration changes that behavioral baseline is the most important unknown. Watch for whether ChatGPT's session length increases after these integrations — that is the metric confirming users are transacting through ChatGPT rather than just getting recommendations.

For Sora specifically, if OpenAI places it behind a paid tier inside ChatGPT, it signals confidence in the product's standalone value. If it's included in the base plan, the goal is habit formation over near-term revenue.
