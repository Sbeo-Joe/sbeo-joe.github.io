## Not a Bubble — A Structural Shift

**Nvidia's earnings announcement rattled markets.** It is a signal that the cyclical profit structure the company enjoyed from surging GPU demand over recent years is no longer functioning as before.

The market is beginning to reassess whether AI-driven revenue growth is a repeating cycle or a one-time structural expansion — and the answer to that question will redefine how the entire tech sector is valued.
